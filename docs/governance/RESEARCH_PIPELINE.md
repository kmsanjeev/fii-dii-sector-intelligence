# RESEARCH PIPELINE

## Project

FII/DII Capital Flow Intelligence Platform

---

# Purpose

The Research Pipeline defines the lifecycle through which new ideas, datasets, indicators, methodologies, intelligence engines, AI capabilities, and platform enhancements move from concept to production.

The objective is to ensure that all development remains evidence-driven, validated, documented, and aligned with the platform vision.

---

# Research Philosophy

Not every idea deserves development.

Every idea must earn its place through validation.

The platform shall prioritize:

Evidence
→ Validation
→ Architecture
→ Development

rather than:

Idea
→ Immediate Coding

---
