# PROJECT SCOPE

## Project Name

Capital Flow Intelligence Platform

---

# Executive Summary

The Capital Flow Intelligence Platform is an end-to-end investment intelligence ecosystem designed to identify how capital moves through financial markets and transform that information into actionable investment opportunities.

Unlike traditional market analysis systems that focus primarily on price movement, this platform focuses on identifying capital movement before it becomes obvious through price action.

The platform is designed to answer a simple but powerful question:

Where is the money flowing?

# Problem Statement

Most investors discover opportunities after significant price movement has already occurred.

Traditional workflows generally follow:

```text
News
    ↓
Price Movement
    ↓
Investor Attention
```

This often results in late participation.

The platform seeks to reverse this process by identifying:

```text
Capital Movement
    ↓
Sector Rotation
    ↓
Theme Rotation
    ↓
Stock Leadership
    ↓
Price Movement
```

before opportunities become widely recognized.
