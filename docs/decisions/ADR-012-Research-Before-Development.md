# ADR-012

## Title

Research Before Development

---

# Status

Accepted

---

# Date

2026-06-03

---

# Context

Feature requests and ideas can rapidly expand project scope.

---

# Decision

All significant development shall follow:

Idea

↓

Research

↓

Validation

↓

Architecture Review

↓

Development

---

# Exceptions

Critical Production Fixes

Security Fixes

Data Integrity Fixes

---

# Benefits

* Reduced Rework
* Better Quality
* Better Resource Allocation

---

# Long-Term Outcome

Platform evolves through validated intelligence rather than assumptions.
