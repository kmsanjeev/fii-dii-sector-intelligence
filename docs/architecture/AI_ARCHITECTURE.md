# AI ARCHITECTURE

## Project

FII/DII Capital Flow Intelligence Platform

---

# Purpose

This document defines the Artificial Intelligence architecture of the platform.

The AI Layer serves as the intelligence delivery system that transforms complex datasets into understandable insights, recommendations, research, portfolio intelligence, and decision support.

AI is not an add-on feature.

AI is a core platform layer.

---

# AI Vision

The long-term vision is to build an AI-powered investment operating system capable of answering:

What is happening?

Why is it happening?

What should I watch next?

What action should I take?

using market intelligence, fundamental intelligence, portfolio intelligence, and execution intelligence.

---

# AI Philosophy

## Principle 1

AI Consumes Intelligence

AI should consume:

* Intelligence Layer
* Signal Layer
* Research Layer

rather than raw data whenever possible.

---

## Principle 2

Explainability First

Every conclusion must be explainable.

Users should understand:

Why

How

What Changed

---

## Principle 3

Evidence Driven

Recommendations must be supported by:

Capital Flow

Fundamentals

Technical Evidence

Historical Context

---

## Principle 4

Human Decision Maker

AI assists.

Human decides.

The platform does not replace investment judgment.
