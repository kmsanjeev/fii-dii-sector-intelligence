# SECTOR INTELLIGENCE

## Project

FII/DII Capital Flow Intelligence Platform

---

# Module Overview

Sector Intelligence is responsible for identifying where capital is flowing at the sector level.

This module acts as the bridge between:

Market Intelligence

and

Theme Intelligence

within the Capital Flow hierarchy.

---

# Capital Flow Hierarchy

```text
Market
    ↓
Sector
    ↓
Theme
    ↓
Stock
```

Sector Intelligence represents the first actionable layer of capital allocation analysis.

---

# Objective

Answer the following questions:

1. Which sectors are attracting capital?
2. Which sectors are losing capital?
3. Which sectors are strengthening?
4. Which sectors are weakening?
5. Which sectors are emerging as leaders?
6. Which sectors offer early opportunities?

---

# Why Sector Intelligence Matters

Institutional capital rarely appears at the stock level first.

Capital often rotates:

Market
→ Sector
→ Theme
→ Stock

before broad market participation recognizes the trend.

The objective of Sector Intelligence is to identify these rotations early.
