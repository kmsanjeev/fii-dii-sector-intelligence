# THEME INTELLIGENCE

## Project

FII/DII Capital Flow Intelligence Platform

---

# Module Overview

Theme Intelligence is responsible for identifying emerging, strengthening, weakening, and declining market themes.

This module acts as the bridge between:

Sector Intelligence

and

Stock Intelligence

within the Capital Flow hierarchy.

---

# Capital Flow Hierarchy

```text
Market
    ↓
Sector
    ↓
Theme
    ↓
Stock
```

Theme Intelligence converts sector-level capital movement into investable narratives.

---

# Objective

Answer the following questions:

1. Which themes are attracting capital?
2. Which themes are emerging?
3. Which themes are accelerating?
4. Which themes are weakening?
5. Which stocks are benefiting from a theme?
6. Which themes represent early opportunities?

---

# Why Theme Intelligence Matters

Sector performance alone is often too broad.

For example:

IT Sector

may contain multiple themes:

* Digital Engineering
* Artificial Intelligence
* SaaS
* Cloud Infrastructure
* Cyber Security

Institutional capital often flows into specific themes before becoming visible across an entire sector.

The purpose of Theme Intelligence is to identify those emerging narratives.
