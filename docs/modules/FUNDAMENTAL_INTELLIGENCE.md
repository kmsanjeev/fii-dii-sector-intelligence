# FUNDAMENTAL INTELLIGENCE

## Project

FII/DII Capital Flow Intelligence Platform

---

# Module Overview

Fundamental Intelligence explains why capital is flowing into or out of a company.

While:

Institutional Intelligence

Sector Intelligence

Theme Intelligence

Stock Intelligence

identify where money is moving,

Fundamental Intelligence identifies the underlying reasons.

---

# Capital Flow Framework

```text
Market
    ↓
Sector
    ↓
Theme
    ↓
Stock
    ↓
Fundamental Reason
```

This module provides the final layer of conviction.

---

# Objective

Answer the following questions:

1. Why is money flowing into a stock?
2. Why is money exiting a stock?
3. What changed fundamentally?
4. Is growth improving or deteriorating?
5. Is management confidence increasing?
6. Is business momentum accelerating?
7. Is the market underestimating future potential?

---

# Why Fundamental Intelligence Matters

Price movement alone is insufficient.

A stock may rise because of:

* Results
* New Orders
* Capacity Expansion
* Management Guidance
* Shareholding Changes
* Regulatory Changes
* Industry Tailwinds

The objective is to identify these drivers before they become consensus.

---

# Intelligence Philosophy

Price reveals behavior.

Fundamentals explain behavior.
