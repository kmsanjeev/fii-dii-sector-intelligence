# GUI PLATFORM

## Project

FII/DII Capital Flow Intelligence Platform

---

# Module Overview

The GUI Platform is the presentation layer of the system.

Its responsibility is to transform complex intelligence into a simple, intuitive, and visually engaging user experience.

The GUI Platform acts as the primary interaction layer between users and all intelligence modules.

---

# Mission

Enable users to understand:

What is happening?

Why is it happening?

What should be monitored?

What should be done next?

within seconds.

---

# Platform Philosophy

---

## Principle 1

Answers Before Data

Users should see conclusions first.

Raw data should support conclusions.

---

## Principle 2

Visualization Before Tables

Prefer:

Heatmaps

Infographics

Maps

Dashboards

Charts

before large datasets.

---

## Principle 3

AI First

The AI Assistant becomes the primary interaction model.

---

## Principle 4

Three-Second Rule

Users should understand current market conditions within three seconds of opening the platform.

---

## Principle 5

Progressive Disclosure

Overview

↓

Sector

↓

Theme

↓

Stock

↓

Details
